Production RAG for a health optimization platform

5,000+ beta users. 8,000+ daily queries. 99.5% uptime.

Architected and scaled a retrieval-augmented generation system serving thousands of users on Google Cloud Run. The first version had a 2.3s response time and shaky retrieval quality. I instrumented the pipeline with AutoRAG, isolated the bottlenecks, then rebuilt retrieval around Weaviate with HNSW + hybrid (dense/sparse) search, overlapping chunking, and a cross-encoder reranker. 2024

BiteWise — AI meal planning that doesn't hallucinate

Top 30 finalist, TED AI hackathon (UN Sustainability Goals).

Android app for tracking dietary habits and getting personalized recipe recommendations. The technical challenge was hallucinations—LLMs love to invent ingredients and confident nutrition numbers. I structured the prompt chain with Chain-of-Thought via LangChain and tight grounding against tracked user data, cutting observed hallucinations by ~98%. Recipes get generated by Gemini, accompanying images by DALL·E 3, all stored in Firebase.
